At Umaang , we help children unlock their potential and realize their dreams. We believe that every child is extraordinary and can scale great heights if provided with the right learning opportunities. With regular and generous contributions from our supporters, we are able to march steadily towards achieving these goals. We aim to keep a smile at every child's face

Save animals and nature beauty

Umaang is dedicated to providing a safe refuge to animals suffering from neglect and cruelty. The purpose is to make people aware about the care, management and nutrition of pet and stray animals. For Umaang, All animals on this planet are the pets of the society and hence we need to realize their importance in our everyday life and do our bit in exchange.
